ALL FOUR

While skiing and filming with my friend Eric Leyland, I wanted to challenge myself to doing all four variations of what is known as a "450 out" (which refers to degrees of spin, coming off of a rail). The four variations being the combinations of leading-left-foot vs leading-right-foot, and blind vs front. The hardest variation was the hard-way front 450, while the most surprising was the hard-way blind, which I landed first try having never attempted it.
This was one of my early attempts at adjusting sound levels, but I hope that I managed to emphasize the sounds of the skis throughout the video. There is something to be said about hearing what is happening, as it happens, in a video.
