2017 SEASON EDIT

This was put together from footage compiled on the last two weekends of the ski season. It was our first opportunity to use the camera stabilizer, DSLR, and mic, to see what kind of quality we could put out. Most of the day was one big learning process, both in figuring out how to best use the stabilizer, and what settings worked best on the camera for filming skiing.
