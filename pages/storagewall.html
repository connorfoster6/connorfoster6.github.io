STORAGE WALL

I used to design specific storage systems, and almost every time I would come to the realization that while the project is neat, it would always fail, because I was not looking at the bigger issue. I realized that what I really wanted was something that could adapt to any scenario, so I took the idea of the rock climbing wall, and turned it into a storage system. This allows me to build onto what I have, as I realize better storage methods. By embracing storage as a functional opportunity, rather than a place to hide our things, we arrive at the opportunity to create natural user interactions, that harmonize us with our environments.
