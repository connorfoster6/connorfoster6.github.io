KEYBOARD APP

WORK IN PROGRESS

Over the past couple years I have gotten fed up with the few keyboards I have tried, as they consistently made typos and inserted words I didn't want. My first phone was a blackberry pearl which had the best phone keyboard I have to date, ever typed on. Granted it was a hardkeyboard, I none the less decided that I would code my own keyboard which drew influence from that Blackberry Pearl. I'm looking at intuitive key layouts, and a custom text prediction engine.
